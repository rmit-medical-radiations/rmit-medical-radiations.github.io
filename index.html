Advancing Medical Radiations Research

Welcome to the landing page for the Medical Radiations Research Group at RMIT University. Our work spans four interconnected disciplines — bringing together clinical expertise, cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to translational research that improves patient outcomes.

We operate within ATRACT, RMIT's centre dedicated to accelerating discoveries from the laboratory to the clinic.
