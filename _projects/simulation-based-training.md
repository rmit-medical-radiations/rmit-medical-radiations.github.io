title: Simulation-Based Training in Medical Imaging
areas: [education, medical-imaging]
status: active
lead: Dr Priya Nair
description: >-
  Designing and evaluating high-fidelity simulation environments for training
  medical imaging students in positioning, protocol selection, and image critique.
---

High-fidelity simulation reduces the pressure on clinical placements while giving students
deliberate-practice opportunities across a broader range of cases than any single site can
provide. This project develops simulation scenarios, assesses learning transfer to the
clinical environment, and evaluates the role of peer feedback in skill acquisition.
