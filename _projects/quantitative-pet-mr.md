  Developing quantitative frameworks for simultaneous PET/MR acquisitions,
  with a focus on attenuation correction and motion compensation.
---

This project investigates the challenges of combining positron emission tomography
with magnetic resonance imaging in a single acquisition session. Key goals include
improving attenuation correction accuracy and reducing motion artefacts that degrade
quantitative accuracy in clinical and research settings.
