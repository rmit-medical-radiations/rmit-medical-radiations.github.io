---
title: Dosimetry Audit for External Beam Radiotherapy
areas: [radiotherapy]
status: completed
lead: Dr James Watkins
description: >-
  A multi-centre audit of end-to-end dosimetry accuracy for external beam
  radiotherapy, benchmarking clinical systems against national reference standards.
---

Accurate dosimetry underpins safe and effective radiotherapy. This completed project
conducted end-to-end audits across participating centres, using calibrated phantom
measurements and independent Monte Carlo calculations to identify systematic deviations
and support centres in resolving discrepancies. Findings contributed to updated national
dosimetry guidance.
