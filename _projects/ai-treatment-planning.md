---

Manual contouring and treatment planning are time-intensive steps in the radiotherapy
workflow. This project trains and validates deep learning models on multi-centre datasets
to automate segmentation of organs at risk and generate dose predictions that planners
can review and approve, accelerating the path from simulation CT to approved plan.
