---

Prostate-specific membrane antigen (PSMA) theranostics pairs a diagnostic radiotracer
with a therapeutic equivalent, enabling treatment selection and response monitoring from
the same molecular target. This project develops patient-specific dosimetry methods and
explores biomarkers of treatment response to support evidence-based clinical protocols.
